About FinanceTracker

Smart Budgeting Made Simple

Track your expenses, manage budgets, and achieve your financial goals as a student.

Features

  • Track unlimited transactions
  • Multi-currency support (RWF, USD, EUR)
  • Budget caps with real-time alerts
  • Advanced regex search functionality
  • Export/Import JSON data
  • Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Full keyboard navigation support
